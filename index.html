Orgada Burger

Orgada Burger is a popular restaurant chain known for its high-quality, handcrafted burgers made with fresh ingredients. They have multiple locations, including branches in Nablus and Ramallah in Palestine, as well as Abu Dhabi in the UAE. Their diverse menu features options like the Classic Beef Burger, Texas Beef Burger, and Mushroom Beef Burger, along with chicken burger meals such as the Supreme Chicken Burger and Chicken Mushroom Burger. Customers often praise the juicy burgers, fresh toppings, and friendly staff.
